[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the shadows falling softly on your face
There is a quiet fire burning in this place
We leave the world behind and lock the wooden door
I only want you closer than I did before

[Verse 1]
The amber light is dancing across the heavy wood
I pull you in my arms exactly where you should

[Pre-Chorus]

[Chorus]

I want to lose myself inside your tender glow
Taking the night easy, taking it nice and slow

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, waking up a deep feeling tonight
Making everything feel so right
